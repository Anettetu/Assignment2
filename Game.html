Health bingo

Do your part in helping spread awarness and protecting your own health. The game provides a variation of tasks that educates you about covid and children health, as well as fun psysical task you can do without any equitment.

Lets play!

In order to win the game you need to complete all the tasks in one row. When you are done you shout Bingo! When the claim is checked by the teacher, you play on trying to be the first one to do all tasks on the board. The first one to finish wins.

Good luck!
