The future in our hands

Spread awareness

This page was created to spread awareness about human health among children and to emphasize the importance of the covid-vaccine.

Why is it important?

The COVID-19 pandemic has treathened the health facilties in many countires. It is now more important than ever that we focus on providing more efficient funding of healt systems, and improving sanitation and hygiene for all.

What can you do to help?

You can start by promoting and protecting your own health and the health of those around you. You can raise awareness in your community about the importance of good health, healthy lifestyles as well as people’s right to quality health care services, especially for the most vulnerable such as women and children.

Kids and covid

Dear children

Are you feeling scared or anxious about the coronavirus? We can understand that. Many children are unsure how the coronavirus will impact their lives, and whether it's dangerous.

Maybe you are afraid of being infected yourself? Or maybe you are scared that someone you know will be infected?

For most children, the coronavirus is harmless. They rarely get symptoms, and if they do, they are experienced as a common cold.

Then why is it important for you?

Eventhough you don't get really sick yourself, you can still infect others. The virus can be dangerous for the elderly, such as your grandparents, or people with underlying diseases.

Remember: It's never anyone's fault if you get infected.

What can you do?

  • Stay home if you are feeling sick
  • Wash your hands when you leave home, and when you get to school
  • Keep a safe distance to others
  • Get vaccinated
